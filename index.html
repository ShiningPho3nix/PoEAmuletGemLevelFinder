How do I calculate gem levels with amulets in Path of Exile?

Use this calculator to find the best amulet modifiers for your gems. Simply search for your skill gem and the tool will show all available modifiers that boost its level, including combinations with Focused Amulet (doubles effects) and Reflecting Mist (1.5x-2.1x multiplier on rare amulets).

What is a Focused Amulet in PoE?

A Focused Amulet is an experimental base type with the implicit "100% increased Explicit Modifier magnitudes". This doubles all explicit modifiers on the amulet. It can only have 2 prefixes and 1 suffix, making it perfect for gem level modifiers since both +gem level prefixes get doubled.

How does Reflecting Mist work with amulets?

Reflecting Mist creates two opposite rare mirrored copies with modifier magnitudes increased by 1.5x to 2.1x randomly. Modifiers are randomly distributed as positive or negative between the copies and can mix on the same item. Works on rare amulets and special bases like Focused Amulet and Simplex Amulet, but not on corrupted items or items with sockets. Obtained from tier 16+ map encounters.

Which damage types work with amulet gem level modifiers?

Amulet modifiers work with Lightning, Fire, Cold, Physical, and Chaos damage gems. There are also universal modifiers that affect all skill gems. You can combine the universal "+1 to all skill gems" with one damage-specific modifier, but cannot have multiple damage-type modifiers on the same amulet.

What's the maximum gem level boost from amulets?

The theoretical maximum is +8 gem levels using a reflected Focused Amulet with two +1 gem level prefixes, where both modifiers get >2.0x multiplier from Reflecting Mist. More realistically, a standard Focused Amulet provides +4 levels (two +1 prefixes × 2 from implicit). Note that negative modifiers may appear on mirrored copies.
